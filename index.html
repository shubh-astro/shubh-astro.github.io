FEATURED PROJECTS

High-Res Spectroscopy of Type II Cepheids

Performing metallicity and metal abundances calculations for Type II Cepheids using SALT and VLT high-res data for precise distance measurements at the first rung of the cosmic ladder.

PySpecTools: High-Res Spectroscopy Pipeline (In development)

Python-based tools for reducing and analyzing echelle stellar spectroscopic data. Future developments involve a module to calculate stellar atmosphere parameters.

GRB Prompt Emission Analysis

Worked on detailed spectral analysis of the prompt emission of GRBs to explore the cause for the observed non-thermal radiation.
